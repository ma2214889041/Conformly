# MDCG 2022-2 — Clinical Evidence for IVDs (模拟摘要)

> ⚠️ Hackathon demo 模拟数据。真实版本请从 https://health.ec.europa.eu/medical-devices-sector/new-regulations/guidance-mdcg-endorsed-documents-and-other-guidance_en 下载。

## 核心内容

1. 临床证据三支柱 (Three Pillars):
   - Scientific Validity (SV) — 分析物与临床状态的关联
   - Analytical Performance (AP) — 分析性能
   - Clinical Performance (CP) — 临床性能

2. 适用于所有 IVD 分类 (A/B/C/D)

3. 与 IVDR Article 56 + Annex XIII 联动
