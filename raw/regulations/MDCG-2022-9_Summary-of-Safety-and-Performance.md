# MDCG 2022-9 — SSP Template (模拟摘要)

适用于 Class C 和 Class D IVD,必须公开发布于 EUDAMED。

## 要求章节

- Device identification & UDI-DI
- Intended purpose
- Risk and warnings
- Summary of clinical evidence
- Possible diagnostic alternatives
- Suggested profile and training for users
- Reference to harmonised standards
